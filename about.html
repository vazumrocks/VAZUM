About VAZUM

Taking the name from a genus of predatory sea snails called Vasum, Zach Pliska launched VAZUM in 2017 with the release of the single Fall Guy. He then recorded three albums VAZUM (2018), Void (2018) and Variant (2019) and began playing locally in Detroit. In 2019 Zach and Emily Sturm met at Leland City Club in Detroit and began writing together. In 2020 they released two albums, Vampyre Villa and Rated and an EP called Vazumnacht. In 2021 they released two more albums, V+ and Unrated V. In 2022 they began touring reginally in the US and continued releasing singles and EPs. In 2023 they relesed anoother album, V- and did more touring, venturing farther into the Southern US.

In 2024 VAZUM expanded their sound with the release of thier album Western Violence, and expanded their reach by touring in the UK and France. VAZUM are currently preparing more releases for 2025 and will be touring the UK during late February / early March 2025.
