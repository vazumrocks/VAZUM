About VAZUM

VAZUM is a dark-rock band from Detroit, MI. Since forming in 2017 they have independently released 3 albums. Their most recent, 'variant' draws on the band's post-punk and goth influences, featuring covers of the Bauhaus classics She's in Parties and The Passion of Lovers. VAZUM is led by Zach Pliska who has previously played drums in a myriad of Detroit bands. Wanting to step to the forefront he sings and plays guitar in VAZUM. Pliska is joined by Zachary Anderson on guitar and Michael O'Connor on bass who both contribute to the songwriting process. The band has a wide range of influences and is further pursuing different directions to take their music.
